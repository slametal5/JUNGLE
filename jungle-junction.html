Jungle Junction

Jungle Junction is an animated children's TV show created by Trevor Ricketts. In the USA, it was part of its Playhouse Disney block. On February 14, 2011, it was moved to its Disney Junior block, which served as Playhouse Disney's replacement. 45 episodes were produced over 2 seasons. The show follows colorful animal vehicles on their adventures. It aired on Disney Channel in the US, and it was produced by British animation company Spider Eye Productions in the UK and it was listed in airing order.





== Characters ==





=== Main ===

Zooter (voiced by Janet James) is a pink pig/scooter who's the jungle messenger. She can zoom around the jungle. She says the title of every Jungle Junction episode. She's Ellyvan's best friend.
