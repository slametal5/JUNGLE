Kai Schreiber

Kai Schreiber (born December 13, 2008) is a British-American model and actress.





== Early life and education ==

Kai Schreiber was born on 13 December 2008. Her father is American actor Liev Schreiber, and her mother is British actress Naomi Watts.

In a 2019 interview, Watts said that both her children developed an interest in acting after enrolling at a summer camp for the performing arts.





== Career ==





=== Acting ===

Schreiber made her film acting debut voicing a young wolf character in the 2016 film The Jungle Book alongside her older brother Sasha Schreiber, who also voiced a young wolf in the film.





=== Modeling ===

In March 2025, Schreiber had her modeling and runway premiere on Alessandro Michele's Valentino fall catwalk at Paris Fashion Week.

Schreiber has been signed to the IMG Models agency.
