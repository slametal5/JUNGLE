Hannah Dunne

Hannah Dunne (born April 8, 1990) is an American actress and model. She is the daughter of actors Griffin Dunne and Carey Lowell, the granddaughter of writer and journalist Dominick Dunne and the niece of actress Dominique Dunne. She is best known for her portrayal of Lizzie Campbell in the Amazon TV series Mozart in the Jungle.
