Adam David Thompson

Adam David Thompson is an American actor known for his roles in Outsiders, Godless, Mozart in the Jungle, and others.





== Career ==

Thompson made his feature film debut opposite Elizabeth Olsen in thriller Martha Marcy May Marlene in 2011. This was followed up by supporting roles in A Walk Among the Tombstones and 10 Things I Hate About Life. He starred opposite Lucy Walters and Shane West in post-apocalyptic film, Here Alone which premiered at the 2016 Tribeca Film Festival and was released theatrically on March 30, 2017.
